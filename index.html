About Us

Welcome to the Ministry of Trade, Industry, MSMEs, Innovation, and Co-operatives. We are dedicated to fostering a vibrant and inclusive business environment, empowering cooperatives, propelling trade and investment, and supporting the growth of micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs).

Our mission is to drive economic development through innovation, sustainable industrialization, and inclusive growth strategies. We collaborate with stakeholders to create policies that promote fair trade practices, encourage entrepreneurship, and enhance the competitiveness of industries.

At the core of our efforts are initiatives that promote technological advancements, foster creativity, and nurture a supportive ecosystem for businesses of all sizes. We believe in the transformative power of partnerships and strive to build strong alliances that drive socio-economic progress.

Explore our website to learn more about our programs, initiatives, and how we are shaping the future of trade, industry, MSMEs, innovation, and cooperatives in our nation.
